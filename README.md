### 🔬 Research Interests

- **Multimodal Behavioral Signals in Clinical Contexts**: My work focuses on using multimodal large language models to identify clinically relevant behavioral markers from patients' verbal and nonverbal behaviors, with the goal of supporting more accurate and comprehensive mental health assessment.

- **The Reliability of Large Language Models in Clinical Psychology**: My research focuses on how large language models infer emotional states in psychologically realistic contexts, particularly in the presence of multimodal and temporally structured data. The goal is to improve the validity, calibration, and interpretability of AI systems for clinical use.
